Exploring the Curonian Spit

The Curonian Spit is a 98 km long, thin, curved sand-dune spit that separates the Curonian Lagoon from the Baltic Sea coast. Its southern portion lies within Kaliningrad Oblast, Russia and its northern within

southwestern Lithuania. It is a UNESCO World Heritage Site shared by the two countries.
